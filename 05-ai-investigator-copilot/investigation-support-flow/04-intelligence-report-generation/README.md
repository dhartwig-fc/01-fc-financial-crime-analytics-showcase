# 04 Intelligence Report Generation

Transforming Investigation Evidence into Actionable Intelligence Products

---

## Executive Summary

Intelligence Report Generation is the fourth operational capability within the AI Investigator Copilot architecture.

The capability transforms alert rationales, investigator summaries, evidence packs, analytical findings, and supporting intelligence into structured intelligence reports suitable for investigative, operational, management, and regulatory consumption.

The objective is to convert large volumes of analytical and investigative information into concise, explainable, decision-ready intelligence products that support financial crime investigations.

By automating report generation, the capability improves consistency, reduces manual documentation effort, and accelerates investigative decision-making.

---

## Capability Objective

The objective of Intelligence Report Generation is to:

- Produce investigation-ready intelligence reports
- Consolidate investigative findings
- Explain risk exposure
- Summarise supporting evidence
- Support investigator decision-making
- Improve reporting consistency
- Reduce report preparation effort
