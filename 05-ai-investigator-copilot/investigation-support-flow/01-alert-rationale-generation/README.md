# 01 Alert Rationale Generation

Transforming Analytics Outputs into Explainable Investigator Intelligence

---

## Executive Summary

Alert Rationale Generation is the first operational capability within the AI Investigator Copilot architecture.

The capability converts analytical outputs, risk scores, typology indicators, network intelligence, and supporting evidence into clear, explainable narratives that help investigators understand why an alert was generated.

Rather than presenting investigators with isolated risk scores or technical model outputs, Alert Rationale Generation provides structured explanations that accelerate investigation and improve decision-making.

---

## Capability Objective

The objective of Alert Rationale Generation is to:

- Explain why an alert was generated
- Summarise key risk indicators
- Highlight supporting evidence
- Translate analytical findings into investigator language
- Improve explainability
- Reduce manual investigation effort
- Support consistent case assessment

### Investigation Inputs

- Counterparty Information
- Jurisdiction Intelligence

---

## Processing Activities

Alert Rationale Generation performs the following functions:

### Risk Interpretation

Interprets analytical risk outputs.

### Evidence Summarisation

Identifies supporting evidence associated with the alert.

### Narrative Generation

Transforms analytical findings into human-readable explanations.

### Investigator Guidance

Provides recommendations on areas requiring review.

---

## Outputs

### Alert Explanation

Explains why the alert was generated.

### Risk Driver Summary

Highlights the primary drivers of risk.

### Evidence Summary

Summarises supporting evidence.

### Investigator Guidance

Identifies recommended review activities.

### Recommended Action

Suggests potential next steps for investigators.

## Governance Considerations

Alert Rationales must:

- Remain explainable
- Be traceable to source evidence
- Avoid unsupported conclusions
- Support investigator review
- Maintain auditability

Generated outputs should always be reviewed by investigators before operational use.
