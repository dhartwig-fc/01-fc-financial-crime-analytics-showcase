# Alert Rationale Generation

## Purpose

Converts risk indicators into clear explanations of why an alert was generated.

## Inputs

- Risk score
- Typology indicators
- Customer information
- Transaction information

## Outputs

- What happened
- Why it matters
- What to review
- Recommended next step
