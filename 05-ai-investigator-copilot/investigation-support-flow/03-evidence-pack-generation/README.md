# 03 Evidence Pack Generation

Transforming Investigation Intelligence into Structured Evidence Packages

---

## Executive Summary

Evidence Pack Generation is the third operational capability within the AI Investigator Copilot architecture.

The capability assembles analytical findings, risk indicators, network intelligence, customer information, transaction data, and supporting documentation into a structured evidence package for investigator review.

The objective is to reduce manual evidence gathering, improve investigative consistency, and provide investigators with a consolidated view of all information relevant to a case.

By organising evidence into a standardised format, the capability improves investigation efficiency and supports explainable decision-making.

---

## Capability Objective

The objective of Evidence Pack Generation is to:

- Consolidate investigation evidence
- Organise supporting information
- Improve evidence accessibility
- Reduce manual preparation effort
- Standardise investigative documentation
- Support case review workflows
- Improve auditability and traceability

## Governance Considerations

Evidence Packs must:

- Maintain source traceability
- Preserve evidence lineage
- Support audit requirements
- Remain evidence-based
- Avoid unsupported conclusions

Evidence should remain linked to originating analytical outputs and source records.
