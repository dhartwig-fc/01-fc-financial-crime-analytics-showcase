# 02 Investigator Summary Generation

Transforming Alert Rationales into Investigator-Ready Briefings

---

## Executive Summary

Investigator Summary Generation is the second operational capability within the AI Investigator Copilot architecture.

The capability transforms alert rationales, analytical findings, risk indicators, network intelligence, and supporting evidence into concise investigator-ready summaries.

The objective is to provide investigators with a clear understanding of suspicious activity, key risk drivers, supporting evidence, and recommended review priorities before detailed investigation begins.

By reducing the time required to interpret analytical outputs, Investigator Summary Generation accelerates case assessment and improves investigative consistency.

---

## Capability Objective

The objective of Investigator Summary Generation is to:

- Summarise suspicious activity
- Consolidate analytical findings
- Highlight key risk indicators
- Prioritise investigative focus areas
- Improve investigator productivity
- Reduce case review effort
- Standardise investigation briefings

## Governance Considerations

Investigator summaries must:

- Remain evidence-based
- Be traceable to analytical findings
- Avoid unsupported conclusions
- Support human review
- Maintain auditability

Generated summaries should be reviewed by investigators before operational use.
