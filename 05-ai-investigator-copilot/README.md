# AI Investigator Copilot

Transforming Financial Crime Analytics into Investigator-Ready Intelligence

---

## Executive Summary

The AI Investigator Copilot capability converts financial crime analytics outputs into structured, investigator-ready intelligence products.

Traditional analytics platforms identify suspicious activity, generate risk scores, and prioritise alerts. However, investigators must still spend significant time reviewing data, interpreting risk indicators, assembling evidence, and preparing case documentation.

The AI Investigator Copilot bridges this gap by transforming analytics outputs into explainable narratives, evidence summaries, investigation reports, and case-ready intelligence products.

This capability sits above all Financial Crime Analytics domains and operationalises risk detection into investigation support.

---

## Capability Objective

The objective of the AI Investigator Copilot is to:

- Explain why an alert was generated
- Summarise risk indicators
- Assemble supporting evidence
- Produce investigator-ready intelligence products
- Accelerate case preparation
- Improve consistency of investigative documentation
- Support human investigator decision-making

The capability is designed to augment investigators rather than replace investigator judgement.
